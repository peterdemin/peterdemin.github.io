# Roles

## * Software Engineer

The main responsibilities and expectations for a * software engineer role will vary depending on the specific company and team. However, here are a few common responsibilities and expectations for this role:

1. Design and develop the company's core software: As a * software engineer, you will be responsible for designing and developing the company's core software. This may include creating technical specifications, writing code, and conducting testing and debugging.
2. Lead the software development team: As a * software engineer, you may also be responsible for leading the software development team, including hiring and managing team members and setting goals and objectives.
3. Collaborate with other teams and departments: As a * software engineer, you will be expected to collaborate with other teams and departments, such as product management, design, and operations, to ensure that the software development team's work aligns with the overall goals and objectives of the company.
4. Stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies: As a * software engineer, you will be expected to stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies, and to share your knowledge and expertise with the team.
5. Help shape the company's engineering culture and approach to software development: As a * software engineer, you may also be expected to help shape the company's engineering culture and approach to software development, and to provide guidance and input on key decisions and strategies.

Overall, the main responsibilities and expectations for a * software engineer are to design and develop the company's core software, lead the software development team, collaborate with other teams and departments, stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies, and help shape the company's engineering culture and approach to software development.

## Senior Software Engineer

The main responsibilities and expectations for a senior software engineer role will vary depending on the specific company and team. However, here are a few common responsibilities and expectations for this role:

1. Design and develop software solutions: As a senior software engineer, you will be responsible for designing and developing software solutions to meet the needs of the business and its customers. This may include creating technical specifications, writing code, and conducting testing and debugging.
2. Collaborate with other teams and departments: As a senior software engineer, you will be expected to collaborate with other teams and departments, such as product management, design, and operations, to ensure that your work aligns with the overall goals and objectives of the company.
3. Mentor and support junior engineers: As a senior software engineer, you may also be expected to mentor and support junior engineers on the team, providing guidance and feedback to help them develop their skills and careers.
4. Stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies: As a senior software engineer, you will be expected to stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies, and to share your knowledge and expertise with the team.
5. Contribute to the company's engineering culture and approach to software development: As a senior software engineer, you may also be expected to contribute to the company's engineering culture and approach to software development, and to provide input and guidance on key decisions and strategies.

Overall, the main responsibilities and expectations for a senior software engineer are to design and develop software solutions, collaborate with other teams and departments, mentor and support junior engineers, stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies, and contribute to the company's engineering culture and approach to software development.
